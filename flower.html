lotus

Lotus, a latinization of Greek lōtos (λωτός), is a genus of flowering plants that includes most bird's-foot trefoils
and deervetches and contains many dozens of species distributed worldwide.
Depending on the taxonomic authority, roughly between 70 and 150 are accepted.

rose

A rose is a woody perennial flowering plant of the genus Rosa,
in the family Rosaceae, or the flower it bears.
There are over three hundred species and tens of thousands of cultivars
. They form a group of plants that can be erect shrubs, climbing, or trailing,
with stems that are often armed with sharp prickles.
Their flowers vary in size and shape and are usually large and showy,
in colours ranging from white through yellows and reds.
